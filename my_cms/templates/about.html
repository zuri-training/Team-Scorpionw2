About MyCMS

MyCMS is a Content Management System which helps users with build responsive websites easily either for personal use or for an organisation. With more than 10 thousand free editable templates readily available, users with little or no experience in coding can build their very own blogs, portfolios, online stores and so on.
what makes MyCMS unique? Our security architecture is unrivaled. A wide range of templates that allow users create, edit, test and copy codes in the easiest way possible. With MyCMS, there is no limit to what you can build. Our goal is to remove any technical or financial barriers that can prevent you from making your own website. Our powerful tools empower individuals and business owners to create a website, sell online, or reach global audiences. Whether you're a beginner or website expert, we're excited to help you on your journey!

Our Mission

Our team started in the web hosting industry. After a few years, we discovered there were too many challenges to making a website. Traditional web hosting services were complicated and expensive to manage. Seeing a need for simplicity, the MyCMS site builder was born. We wanted to offer a website building platform that would require no coding skills or design experience, with everything needed to get published online. You can simply focus on creating an amazing website that reflects your brand. After seeing an increased need for ecommerce solutions, we developed one of the only fully-featured, free and commission-free online store builders. As our global userbase grew, we expanded our editor with the ability to make multilingual websites.
